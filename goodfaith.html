Good Faith Use

Decision Authority Research (DAR) is a governance methodology. It is designed to clarify responsibility before irreversible decisions occur.

It aligns with regulatory expectations for human oversight and accountability, but does not constitute legal advice, compliance certification, or protection from liability.

The responsibility for decisions remains with the organization and its designated individuals.

DAR defines who is accountable to stop.

It does not replace judgment.
