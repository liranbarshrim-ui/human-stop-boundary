Decision Authority Research

What it is

A governance framing for high-stakes decisions under uncertainty.

Core principle

When a decision becomes irreversible under uncertainty, a single named person must have the authority to stop it.

Not a role. Not a committee. A name.

What it does

Maps critical outcomes to one named individual before failure. Not accountability after the fact. Named commitment before execution becomes impossible to reverse.

Position

Not cybersecurity. Not AI governance. Not compliance.

One question: who is named to stop an irreversible decision?

What it is not
A regulatory certification
Legal advice
A software product
Status

Independent governance research. Applied in practice.
