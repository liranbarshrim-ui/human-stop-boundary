What stopping means in practice

In high-stakes systems, the question is not whether controls exist. It is whether someone is defined to stop execution before it becomes irreversible.

DAR defines:

— The irreversibility threshold
— One named individual
— The moment authority must be exercised

After an incident, accountability is always assigned.
The only question is whether it was defined in advance.
