Decision Accountability Record (DAR)

A Governance Standard Proposal for Human Stop Authority in Autonomous Systems
PRINCIPLE

The Authority to Initiate is not the Authority to Terminate.

Systems may execute decisions. Accountability remains human.

DEFINITION

A Decision Accountability Record (DAR) records the named individual who holds the authority to stop a system before execution becomes irreversible.

REFERENCE CASE

The 2012 Knight Capital event demonstrated that catastrophic failures can occur when no single person holds the authority to stop execution.
