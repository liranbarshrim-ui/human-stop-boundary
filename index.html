THE PRINCIPLE

The authority to initiate is not the authority to terminate.

Autonomous systems may execute decisions. Responsibility remains human.

The gap between information and named stop authority is not a bug.

It is a structural condition.

DEFINITION

Assigns a named human authority responsible for stopping automated systems before irreversible execution occurs.

If you cannot name the person, there is no one to stop it.
